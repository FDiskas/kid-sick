Privacy Policy

Kid Sick Tracker - Last updated: March 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Kid Sick Tracker handles your information. Kid Sick Tracker is a frontend-only web app used to track child health records such as temperature logs, medication logs, growth records, and notes.

1. Information You Provide

You may enter the following data into Kid Sick Tracker:

  • Child profile information (name, birth date, optional notes)
  • Temperature entries (date/time, value, unit, method, notes)
  • Medication entries (date/time, medication, dose, notes)
  • Growth entries (date/time, height, weight, notes)
  • General child notes

2. How Data Is Stored

Kid Sick Tracker stores your records in a Google Sheet in your own Google Drive account. The app uses your Google authorization to create, read, and update that spreadsheet on your behalf.

We do not run a dedicated backend server for this app, and we do not maintain a separate central database for your health logs.

3. Google Account Access

The app uses Google Sign-In and Google APIs (such as Google Drive and Google Sheets) only to authenticate you and access the spreadsheet used by Kid Sick Tracker.

Access tokens are handled in the browser session so the app can perform requested sheet operations.

4. Data Sharing

Kid Sick Tracker does not sell your data and does not share your child health logs with third parties, except as required to use Google APIs for the core spreadsheet functionality.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your records remain in your Google Sheet until you delete them. You can remove entries from within the app, or delete the spreadsheet directly from your Google Drive.

6. Security

We use the security mechanisms provided by Google authentication and Google Drive permissions. You are responsible for maintaining control of your Google account and device access.

7. Children and Sensitive Data

Kid Sick Tracker may contain sensitive health-related notes entered by caregivers. Please avoid entering unnecessary personally identifying information and use the app only in accordance with local privacy and health-data requirements that apply to you.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the app evolves. The latest version will be available on this page with an updated effective date.
